I am a current student at Portland State University pursuing my degree in Computer Science. My goal is to become a web developer.

I graduated with a B.F.A in Studio Art in December 2017, during which I developed interest in the intersection between art and technology. My professional experience includes 5 years within museum and video production settings, where I worked with camera and lighting equipment and creative software, assisted with website maintenance and design, taught workshops, and participated as an artist in shows exploring technology as a medium.

My current projects include research for the Jordan Schnitzer Museum of Art's upcoming show Weaving Data, which explores the relationship between weaving and computer science, and the XRchive project organized with the Creative and Emergent Technology Institute, which will use augmented reality to bring light to stories of historical migrations and displacements within Portland.
